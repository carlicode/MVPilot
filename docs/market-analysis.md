## 🚨 Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: AWS Free Tier Limitations

**Risk:** Users exceed Free Tier, incur unexpected costs

**Mitigation:**
- Clear usage warnings in UI
- Auto-pause at 80% of limits
- Upgrade prompts with cost estimates
- Cost calculator tool

### Risk 2: AI Generation Quality

**Risk:** Generated MVPs have bugs or poor quality

**Mitigation:**
- Extensive template testing
- AI code review layer
- User feedback loop
- Manual review for enterprise tier

### Risk 3: Competitive Response

**Risk:** Bubble, Webflow, v0 add similar features

**Mitigation:**
- Speed to market (first-mover advantage)
- Multi-agent moat (complex to replicate)
- AWS partnership (exclusive features)
- Community & network effects

### Risk 4: Market Education

**Risk:** Users don't understand multi-agent concept

**Mitigation:**
- Simple messaging: "Your AI tech team"
- Video tutorials & demos
- Onboarding flow with examples
- Success stories & case studies

### Risk 5: Scaling Costs

**Risk:** AWS costs grow faster than revenue

**Mitigation:**
- Usage-based pricing
- Optimize Lambda & Bedrock calls
- Caching & batching strategies
- Enterprise tier for high-volume users
